outdoor dining

We are delighted to announce that from 12th April 2021, our beautiful hotel gardens will be open for outside dining and refreshments.

Enjoy delicious dishes from our spring menu, excellent coffee and indulgent Afternoon Tea from Laura Ashley The Tea Room on our lawns. A full bar service will be available, serving premium beers, cocktails, prosecco, and champagne along with an extensive wine list.

  • Food served from 12.00-2.00pm & 5.30-9.00pm, weather permitting. Hotel guests can also choose to dine in the privacy of their room.
  • Afternoon Tea available Thursday – Sunday, 12.00-4.00pm, outside only.

Advanced booking is required. To make a reservation, contact us directly on 01628 429955.

Contact Us

We invite you to join us at our table, where the country manor ambiance is as comforting as the cuisine. Classic design and modern culinary style merge seamlessly at our 2AA Rosette awarded Oak Restaurant, the signature fine-dining restaurant in Buckinghamshire.

Here, the finest ingredients are carefully blended to create an elegant and distinctive gastronomic experience showcasing traditional British specialities. Utterly reflective of our peaceful Buckinghamshire surroundings, the Oak Restaurant offers a timeless setting for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

For a more casual affair, join us at Brassiere 1727 – the perfect spot for lunch or a delicious dining experience.

Private dining rooms are available on request (subject to charge) for special occasions or family get-togethers.
